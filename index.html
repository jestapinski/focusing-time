FAQ

Quick answers about this productivity timer.

How does this improve efficiency?

You work in focused intervals with clear boundaries, making it easier to avoid multitasking and protect the time needed for deep work.

What focus length should I start with?

Start with 25 minutes and adjust based on your energy and task complexity. Many people move to 45-60 minute blocks for deep work.

Does the timer keep my progress?

Yes. It stores settings and recent sessions locally in your browser so you can keep a consistent routine.

Is Focusing Time free?

Yes. The timer is free to use in your browser.
